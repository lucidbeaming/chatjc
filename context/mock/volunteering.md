# Volunteering

## Code Mentors PDX — Workshop Instructor
**Volunteer, 2021–Present · Portland, OR**

Anon volunteers as a workshop instructor for Code Mentors PDX, a nonprofit that teaches programming fundamentals to underserved high school students in the Portland area.

### Role and Contributions
- Leads monthly Python workshops for groups of 10–15 students
- Developed a beginner-friendly curriculum covering variables, loops, functions, and basic web scraping
- Mentors individual students on personal projects, including a weather dashboard and a simple chatbot
- Helped three students earn scholarships to local coding bootcamps

### Recognition
Program director Lisa Trần noted: "Anon has a gift for making technical concepts accessible. Students who were intimidated by coding leave their workshops excited to keep learning. Their patience and enthusiasm make a real difference."
